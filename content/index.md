# Hello!

I'm MP. I'm using this as my website, blog, and a data dump (_to be perfectly honest xdd_) most probably for my own **personal** use, but also if I wanna send someone anything probably. Also if you couldn't tell I spent waaaay too much time setting this up, and as a side note, I copied almost everything from SkiFreak's site so do what you will with that information. 

If you've decided to click on the [[big blue hypertext]] in the corner of the website then welcome! This is essentially most of everything that I find interesting on the web and it's essentially an accumulation of everything I find, and might come in handy if I need to reuse some work on another device anywhere else. If for some reason you're here and you don't know me, that's probably because you stalked or stumbled my GitHub, a bit weird to be honest... you do you though.

I'll usually attach any rants, long essays or anything of use to me that I have managed to write. This'll probably be a decently large site being an archive of a lot of things I have written, luckily Quartz 4 came to save us all from having to pay for obsidian sync, but hey, cool that I can share stuff with people.

If you are interested in making you're own 'digital garden', I highly recommend checking out the [Quartz website](https://quartz.jzhao.xyz/), which also has a video guide on how to set up Quartz. Having a smooth brain like mine is ==not recommended== whilst setting up the web page (unless you're masochistic), it'll probably take well over 1h 30m out of your life, else, some common sense is more than recommended, *along with some knowledge in JavaScript*.
