## Old British Coins

First coins used were gold **Staters**. From ancient Gaul. 150 BC.

Production in Britain of coins 100 BC. Gold, silver, bronze.

Roman coins circulated along with the native coins in 45 AD.

**Aureus** - gold, **Denarius** - silver, **Sestertius** - silver, **As** - bronze

Silver **Penny** 410 AD, continued to be called Denarius (1d) up until 1971, when it was abolished in the decimalisation of 1971.
